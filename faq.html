Frequently Asked Questions
Have questions? We're here to help.
What is the iDoc App and what features does it offer?

iDoc is a comprehensive healthcare app designed to offer users a variety of health and wellness features. It includes a Health Check Kiosk to monitor vital health parameters, a Symptom Checker to help understand health conditions, a Medical Encyclopedia for enhancing health literacy, and a Health and Fitness feature that provides nutritional data, custom diet plans, workout plans, and a gym locator.

How does the Health Check Kiosk feature work?

The Health Check Kiosk feature allows users to monitor crucial health vitals such as glucose levels, BMI, height, weight, uric acid levels, oxygen saturation, heart rate, and blood pressure. Run the tests on the kiosk and get your parameters into the app and receive real-time, reliable results.

What symptoms can be checked using the Symptom Checker feature?

Our Symptom Checker allows users to input a wide range of symptoms related to various health conditions. Whether common ailments or less familiar conditions, the Symptom Checker provides immediate and reliable health insights.

What information can I find in the Medical Encyclopedia feature?

The Medical Encyclopedia feature provides a comprehensive library of medical content covering a wide range of diseases and conditions. Users can enhance their health literacy by accessing this vast repository of information anytime, anywhere.

How can the Health and Fitness feature assist in my wellness journey?

Our Health and Fitness feature is an all-in-one companion for a healthier lifestyle. It provides nutritional data for over 100,000 food items, a variety of healthy recipes, custom diet plans tailored to your dietary preferences, personalized workout plans, and a gym locator to discover gyms in your vicinity. This comprehensive feature combines nutrition, fitness, and convenience to empower your wellness journey.

How accurate is the information provided by iDoc app?

iDoc is designed to provide accurate and reliable health information. However, it's important to remember that it doesn't replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. It's always recommended to consult with a healthcare professional for any health concerns.

How is my data protected in the iDoc app?

iDoc prioritizes user data security. We comply with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) to ensure data privacy and security provisions for safeguarding medical information.

How can I get started with iDoc?

Download the iDoc app from either the Google Play Store or Apple App Store, register your account, and start using the variety of features available to enhance your health and wellness journey. If you need assistance or have any questions, our support team is always here to help.

Is the iDoc app free to use?

Yes, iDoc is free to use. However, certain premium features and services may be available at an additional cost.

What can I do if I can't understand a certain medical term in the Medical Encyclopedia?

The Medical Encyclopedia is designed to be user-friendly and accessible. However, if you encounter a term you don't understand, feel free to reach out to our support team for further clarification.

How often is the nutrition and recipe data updated in the Health and Fitness feature?

The nutrition and recipe data in the Health and Fitness feature is regularly updated to ensure accuracy and to provide a wide variety of options for our users.

Can I access the iDoc app on multiple devices?

Yes, you can access your iDoc account on multiple devices. Simply log in with your credentials on any device you wish to use.

How does the Symptom Checker work?

The Symptom Checker works by asking you to input your symptoms into our easy-to-use interface. It then uses AI and machine learning to analyze these symptoms and delivers potential health conditions that align with your inputs.

Can I request custom diet plans from nutrition experts via the app?

Yes, the Health and Fitness feature of iDoc includes the provision for users to request custom diet plans from nutrition experts.

How does the 'Nearby Gym' feature work?

The 'Nearby Gym' feature uses your location data to locate gyms in your vicinity. You can check details like timing, pricing, and contact details for these gyms.

Can I use the iDoc app for emergency medical situations?

While iDoc provides a host of valuable health tools, it should not be used for emergency medical situations. In case of a health emergency, please call your local emergency number or seek immediate medical attention.

How does the app ensure the accuracy of health vitals data from the Health Check Kiosk feature?

The Health Check Kiosk feature is designed with advanced algorithms to provide accurate and reliable health vitals data. However, it's always best to consult a healthcare professional if you notice any significant changes in your health vitals.

What languages is the iDoc app available in?

The iDoc app is primarily available in Arabic and English. However, we're continually working to add more languages to make our app accessible to users worldwide.
