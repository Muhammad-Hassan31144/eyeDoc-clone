Health Check Kiosk

Introducing the Health Check Kiosk feature in our iDoc healthcare app,
bringing convenience to your healthcare journey. Strategically located at
local stores, pharmacies, and malls, our kiosks provide real-time vitals like
heart rate, blood pressure, and more. Access results instantly on the iDoc
app, making health monitoring effortless and accessible anywhere.

Symptom Checker

Navigating through health concerns has never been easier with our state-of-the-art Symptom Checker feature. Designed with the user in mind, this feature offers you the convenience of a preliminary medical consultation at your fingertips.
Our Symptom Checker is your personal, digital diagnostician. It empowers you to identify potential health conditions based on the symptoms you are experiencing, paving the way for timely medical attention and better health outcomes.

Medical Encyclopedia

Empower your healthcare journey with our comprehensive Medical
Encyclopedia feature, a reservoir of knowledge and a trusted companion for
your health-related queries. Our feature is designed to

Our Medical Encyclopedia serves as your personal, digital library, featuring a

Exercises

Unleash your potential and step up your fitness game with our dynamic Exercise Workouts feature. This feature transforms your device into a personal fitness trainer, offering you a variety of workouts tailored to your specific needs and goals. Our Exercise Workouts feature is your comprehensive fitness companion, providing you with a selection of exercises across various disciplines, from cardio and strength training to yoga and flexibility workouts.

Nutritions and Recipe Data

Revolutionize the way you approach your diet with our Nutritions and
Recipe Data feature. This feature is a comprehensive tool that provides

Our Nutritions and Recipe Data feature is like having a personal dietitian and

Custom Diet Plan

We understand that when it comes to health and nutrition, one size does not fit all. With our unique Custom Diet Plan feature, users can now benefit from personalized meal strategies tailored to their individual goals and preferences.

  • iDoc connects users with certified nutrition experts who assess individual dietary needs based on personal health data, lifestyle, dietary preferences, and health goals. Whether you're looking to lose weight, gain muscle, manage a medical condition, or simply improve your overall well-being, our nutrition experts can devise a diet plan tailored just for you.
  • The process is simple and convenient. After users input their dietary preferences, health data, and goals, our nutrition experts go to work, creating a custom diet plan. This plan not only incorporates a variety of nutrient-rich foods to promote overall health but also considers personal food preferences to ensure the plan is enjoyable and sustainable.
  • These diet plans are easily accessible within the app, and users can track their progress and adapt the plan based on the changes in their health statistics and goals. Users also have the option to consult with nutrition experts within the app, enabling real-time adjustments to the diet plan.
  • By merging health technology with professional nutritional expertise, our Custom Diet Plan feature makes personalized nutrition accessible and practical, putting you on a path towards achieving your health goals. It's like having a personal nutritionist, right at your fingertips!

Nearby Gym

Staying active and finding the right fitness facility shouldn't be a chore. With our innovative Nearby Gym feature, users can conveniently discover gyms and fitness centers located around their current location.

  • Our app does the hard work for you, identifying the proximity of gyms, providing key information such as operation hours, pricing, and contact details. It's like having a personal fitness concierge service in your pocket! This feature is designed to support users in maintaining an active lifestyle by making gym access as effortless as possible.
  • Users can peruse through gym profiles and make informed decisions based on provided details. Each gym profile includes comprehensive information such as types of equipment available, variety of classes offered, special facilities such as pools or saunas, and even user ratings and reviews. This allows users to choose a gym that aligns with their fitness goals, preferences, and budget.
  • Moreover, real-time navigation features make it easier for users to locate the gym, eliminating the hassle of searching for directions. The app also provides users with the option to contact the gym directly through the integrated contact features, facilitating inquiries and membership registrations.
  • Our Nearby Gym feature streamlines the process of finding the right fitness environment for our users, proving that no matter where you are, your fitness journey can continue uninterrupted. Fitness made accessible, that's our goal
