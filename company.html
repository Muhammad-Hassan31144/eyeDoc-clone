Overview

At iDoc, we're revolutionizing healthcare through our innovative, free-to-use mobile app called "iDoc"

Our primary mission is to make healthcare more accessible, convenient, and personalized, breaking barriers and fostering a healthier global community. Our all-in-one app comes power-packed with distinctive features.

Our offerings go beyond the features. We are dedicated to cultivating lasting relationships with our users, understanding their unique needs and medical histories.

We view healthcare as a journey, and we aim to walk this path with our users, providing tailored care that truly meets their individual requirements.

At iDoc, we're more than just a healthcare app; we're a partner in your health and wellness journey, committed to making healthcare more personal, more accessible, and more aligned with your needs.

Our Vision

At iDoc, our vision is to democratize healthcare, making it universally accessible and personally tailored. We strive to innovate continuously, utilizing technology to make health management as convenient and intuitive as possible.

We envision a future where healthcare transcends physical boundaries, providing everyone with the personalized care they need right at their fingertips.

Our commitment to this vision drives us to create solutions like our iDoc app, designed to empower individuals in their health and wellness journey.

Our Mission

By creating our comprehensive "iDoc" app, we aim to provide a robust platform where users can manage their health at their convenience.

Our array of features - from the Health Check Kiosk and Symptom Checker, to the Medical Encyclopedia, and the Health and Fitness suite - are designed to cater to diverse health needs.

We are dedicated to simplifying health management and empowering individuals to take control of their wellness journey. With iDoc, we're paving the way for a future where healthcare is always within reach.

Our Technology

Platform Availability

At iDoc, we leverage advanced technology to bridge the gap between healthcare and convenience. Our iDoc app, available on both Android and iOS platforms, is built on cutting-edge technology stacks - Java for Android and Swift for iOS, ensuring a seamless and intuitive user experience.

Responsive Interface

Our robust backend technology ensures efficient data processing while the frontend offers a visually appealing and user-friendly interface. We employ reliable data management and storage, ensuring fast, efficient, and secure access to health data.

Real-time Communication

Harnessing the power of WebRTC (Web Real-Time Communication) technology, our app facilitates real-time communication and video conferencing, providing an interactive platform for users to connect with healthcare providers seamlessly.

Data Security

At the core of our operation is a commitment to data security and privacy. Our use of HTTPS guarantees secure data transmission, while our robust user authentication mechanisms ensure only authorized access to sensitive health information. Our services are in strict compliance with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA), further cementing our commitment to protecting user data.

AI Based Features

Powering our Symptom Checker feature is AI-based technology, which sifts through possible diagnoses to provide accurate health insights. The Medical Encyclopedia feature is optimized for easy reading and sharing of reliable medical content. With iDoc, cutting-edge technology meets healthcare to provide a transformative user experience.
